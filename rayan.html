A-Maua.

A-Maua was founded in 1965 by a group of flower enthusiasts who wanted to provide beautiful, high-quality flowers to their local community. The company started out as a small flower shop in a quaint downtown area, selling fresh-cut flowers, potted plants, and custom arrangements. Over the years, A-Maua grew in popularity, thanks in part to its commitment to sourcing the freshest flowers from local growers and its dedication to personalized customer service. The company expanded to include a greenhouse, where it began growing its own flowers and plants, and a flower delivery service, which allowed customers to have beautiful bouquets delivered straight to their door. In the 1980s, A-Mua began to focus more on sustainable and eco-friendly practices, working to reduce waste and minimize its environmental impact. The company also started offering classes and workshops, teaching customers how to arrange their own flowers and create beautiful centerpieces for special occasions. As the internet became more popular in the 1990s, A-Maua launched its own website, allowing customers to browse its products and place orders online. The company also began to expand its reach, shipping flowers and plants across the country. A-Maua is a well-known and respected name in the floral industry, with multiple locations across the country and a loyal customer base. The company continues to prioritize sustainability and eco-friendliness, and remains committed to providing the highest-quality flowers and personalized customer service to its customers.
